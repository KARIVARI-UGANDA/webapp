WHO WE SERVE

Built for three kinds of traveller

We don't try to be everything to everyone. Karivari is built for travellers who want their transport handled by people who understand exactly what their trip needs.

camera

Photography

For travellers carrying serious gear. Open-roof vehicles, early starts, owner-drivers who understand light and patience. Trips usually run ten to fourteen days.

forest

Wildlife & Safari

For gorilla tracking, big game in the savannah parks, and birding along the Albertine Rift. Long park transfers, reliable vehicles, drivers who double as guides. Seven to fourteen day trips.

hotel_class

Luxury

For travellers booking premium lodges who expect their transport to match. Quiet vehicles, polished service, end-to-end coordination with your itinerary.

HOW IT WORKS

From booking to dropoff

Three steps. No middlemen.

1
Choose your vehicle

Browse our verified fleet. Filter by trip type, dates, and pickup location. Every listed vehicle has been inspected.

2
Book and confirm

Pay one daily rate plus a €2 service fee. You receive written confirmation with insurance details and your owner-driver's contact.

3
Travel

Your owner-driver meets you at the airport or your hotel. They drive, they guide, they handle the road. You travel.

WHY KARIVARI

Why travellers choose Karivari

Three things that no other option in Uganda combines.

Verified, not random

The driver who meets you at the airport is the same person who owns the vehicle. We have inspected the car, checked the insurance, and trained the driver. You are not getting a stranger from an app.

Built for safaris, not city rides

Uber and Bolt are designed for short, urban trips. They were not built for ten-day journeys across national parks with photography gear in the back. Karivari was.

A real person if something goes wrong

Onsite offices in Kampala and Entebbe. 24/7 phone, WhatsApp, and email. You always have someone to call who is actually in Uganda.
