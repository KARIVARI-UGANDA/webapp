Your Journey in Detail

1
Create a Free Account

Sign up in under 60 seconds with your email. No credit card required until you book.

2
Search & Filter

Enter your pickup location, travel dates, and preferred vehicle type. Use price sliders and feature filters to narrow down your perfect match.

3
Review the Listing

Read owner reviews, inspect the photo gallery, check the vehicle spec sheet, and confirm availability for your exact dates.

4
Pay Securely

Complete checkout via Mobile Money (MTN / Airtel) or card. Payment is held in escrow — the owner is only paid after successful pickup.

5
Pick Up the Vehicle

Meet your driver at your agreed point. Do a quick walk-around inspection together, confirm the condition in the app, and the trip begins.

6
Return & Review

Return the vehicle, settle any extra charges, and leave a review to help the community. Your deposit is released automatically.
