FAQ

Common questions from owner-drivers

What type of vehicle do I need? expand_more

We list 4×4 vehicles only — Land Cruisers, Prados, Safari vans, and similar models that are suited for Uganda's terrain. Your vehicle must be 2005 or newer, in good mechanical condition, and have valid insurance and a roadworthy certificate.

How much can I earn? expand_more

Earnings depend on your vehicle type and availability. Most active owner-drivers complete 8–15 trips per month during peak season. At an average daily rate of UGX 350,000 and keeping 82%, that is between UGX 2.3M and UGX 4.3M per month from Kari Vari bookings alone.

Do I need to speak German? expand_more

Basic conversational German is an advantage and is part of our driver orientation. We provide a short language guide. Many of our travellers also speak English, and our office in Germany handles pre-trip communication so you can focus on the driving.

What if something goes wrong on a trip? expand_more

Call our 24/7 support line. We coordinate with our roadside partner network and will dispatch assistance or a replacement vehicle within 4 hours for mechanical breakdowns. Towing is covered for mechanical faults — not accidents. Insurance on the booking covers CDW.

Can I cancel a booking after accepting it? expand_more

Cancellations by owners within 7 days of the trip start date affect your reliability score and may result in a temporary suspension from new bookings. We understand genuine emergencies happen — contact support as soon as possible and we will work with you on a resolution.

Ready to put your vehicle to work?

Applications are reviewed within 48 hours. If you're approved, you can be taking bookings within a week.
